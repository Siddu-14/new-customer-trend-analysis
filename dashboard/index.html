🔍 Key Business Insights & Recommendations

  • Gender Revenue Gap: Male customers generate ~68% of total revenue ($157,890 vs $75,191). Consider targeted marketing campaigns for female segments to balance revenue streams.
  • Age Group Opportunity: The 55+ age group contributes the highest revenue ($69,590), followed by 25-34. Loyalty programs should prioritize these high-value cohorts.
  • Customer Loyalty: 80% of customers are classified as Loyal (3,116), with only 83 New customers. Focus on acquisition strategies to grow the funnel.
  • Subscription Impact: Non-subscribers outnumber subscribers 2.7:1 and contribute 73% of revenue. Incentivizing subscriptions could improve retention.
  • Discount Strategy: 43% of transactions use discounts, with Hats and Sneakers seeing ~50% discount rates. Evaluate margin impact vs. volume uplift.
  • Shipping Preferences: Express shipping customers spend slightly more ($60.48) than Standard ($58.46). Promote Express for higher AOV.
  • Product Quality: Gloves, Sandals, and Boots have the highest review ratings (>3.82). Leverage these as flagship products in campaigns.
  • Repeat Buyer Behavior: Among customers with >5 previous purchases, 72% are not subscribed — a major upsell opportunity for subscription services.
